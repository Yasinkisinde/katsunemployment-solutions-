How does KATS Unemployment Solutions help job seekers?

KATS Unemployment Solutions provides comprehensive job placement services including:

  • Career counseling and guidance
  • Job matching based on your skills and experience
  • Resume/CV writing assistance
  • Interview preparation and coaching
  • Access to exclusive job opportunities
  • Follow-up support after placement

We work with a wide network of employers across various industries in Uganda to connect you with the right opportunities.

What are the requirements to register as a job seeker?

To register with KATS Unemployment Solutions as a job seeker, you'll need:

  • Updated CV/resume
  • Copies of your academic certificates and transcripts
  • Professional certifications (if applicable)
  • National ID or passport
  • Recent passport-sized photograph

How long does the job placement process take?

The job placement timeline varies depending on several factors:

  • Your qualifications and experience: Highly skilled candidates typically get placed faster
  • Industry demand: Some sectors have more openings than others
  • Location preferences: Being open to different locations increases opportunities
  • Salary expectations: Realistic expectations lead to faster placements

On average, most candidates secure employment within 2-10 working days after handing in your profile, though some specialized roles may take longer.

Do you offer internship opportunities?

Yes, we provide internship placement services for both students and recent graduates:

  • For Students: We partner with companies to arrange academic internships that fulfill university requirements

What should I do if I'm not satisfied with a placement?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're unhappy with a placement:

  1. Contact us immediately: Reach out to your consultant within the first week
  2. Explain the issue: Be specific about what isn't working
  3. Allow us to mediate: We'll communicate with the employer to resolve concerns, or shift you to another opportunity
